# Project Thesis & Core Principles

**Status:** CANONICAL. This document defines the intellectual core of VAR Room.

---

## 1. The Core Thesis

**Football's most contested decisions are unresolved not because the data is missing, but because FIFA Law deliberately requires human judgment no camera or dataset can replace.**

This is not a limitation of officiating or technology. It is the law functioning as designed.

### 1.1 The Two Proof Mechanisms
The law preserves this required judgment space through two architectural mechanisms:
1. **Undefined Terms:** Words that carry decisive legal weight with no formal definition (e.g., "deliberately," "unnaturally bigger," "serious foul play").
2. **Deliberate Silence:** Concepts the law could address but intentionally omits (e.g., the adequacy of a remedy in extreme context).

---

## 2. The Experience Architecture

The thesis is not asserted through text. It is proved through a participatory mechanism:

**The Two-Decision Mechanic (Experience Before Explanation):**
1. **Decision 1:** The user judges an incident before receiving legal context.
2. **Perspectives:** The user encounters five divergent AI perspectives grounded in evidence.
3. **The Reveal:** The user sees the exact FIFA Law text and discovers the term has no definition (or the law is deliberately silent).
4. **Decision 2:** The user judges again, realizing their certainty was built on undefined terms.

Sequence: `Decision → Experience → Discovery → Explanation → Reflection`

---

## 3. The Project Principles (Locked)

1. **The Thesis Is the Product:** Every feature must serve the core claim. If removing a feature does not make the thesis harder to prove, the feature does not belong.
2. **Experience Before Explanation:** Deliver the experience (the decision and controversy) before explaining the law.
3. **Specificity Is the Proof:** Anchor claims to specific, verifiable words in specific FIFA Laws, attached to specific incidents. Never rely on abstract claims like "refereeing is hard."
4. **Genuine Divergence:** The five perspectives must reach genuinely opposing conclusions based on valid, cited evidence.
5. **Legal-Textual Accuracy:** Every FIFA Law citation must be verified against the official text. Zero tolerance for hallucinated law.
6. **The Demo Is the Product:** A broken live demo is a fatal failure. The judging demo must use pre-computed, zero-risk outputs.
7. **One Moment, Not Many Features:** Optimize for a single memorable, quotable insight that survives a week of judge memory.
8. **Participatory Discovery:** The user must act and discover, not passively read.
9. **Granite Must Be Structurally Necessary:** If replacing Granite with a static lookup table would not visibly degrade the multi-perspective argumentation capability, Granite is merely decorative.
10. **No Strategic Drift:** Do not introduce new features, theories, or incidents without explicit new evidence that breaks the locked concept.
