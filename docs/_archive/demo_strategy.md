# Final Demo Strategy & Pacing

**Status:** CANONICAL. This defines the exact 3-minute flow, optimized for recall and impact over completeness.

---

## 1. The Core Objective
The demo exists to produce 2-3 moments a judge will remember a week later. It does not exist to demonstrate every feature, every category, or every permutation.

## 2. The Three Must-Land Beats
1. **Perišić Reveal:** "The word 'deliberately' appears four times in Law 12 and has no definition." (The primary proof).
2. **De Jong Proof:** "The referee gave yellow. Years later, watching the same footage, he said it should have been red." (The unassailable specific evidence).
3. **Suárez Closer:** "Every rule was followed. Ghana still went home." (The emotional climax).

## 3. The 3-Minute Narrative Flow

**0:00–0:10 | Hook**
"Football's biggest controversies aren't about what happened. They're about what a word means."

**0:10–0:45 | Perišić (Intent/Threshold)**
Setup → Decision 1 → Divergence (Fan vs. Player) → Reveal: "Deliberately" has no definition. "Unnaturally bigger" has no definition. → Decision 2.

**0:45–1:05 | VAR Nested Layer (Judgment)**
"The referee initially said no penalty. VAR told him to look again. But VAR can only act for a 'clear and obvious error.' That phrase has no definition either. The correction mechanism has the same problem."

**1:05–1:45 | De Jong (Threshold)**
Setup → Decision 1 (3 options) → Reveal: Webb gave yellow, later said red. Same referee, same footage, two answers. → Reveal: "Reckless" vs. "excessive force" has no defined boundary. → Decision 2.

**1:45–2:00 | Mbappé (Scope)**
Compressed Setup: "Deliberately played" resets offside. → Reveal: "Deliberately played" has no definition, nor do its guidance terms. Undefined all the way down. → Decision 2.

**2:00–2:45 | Suárez (Context)**
Transition: "The first three incidents showed you terms the law uses but doesn't define. This last one is different."
Setup → Decision 1 ("Was justice served?") → Reveal: "The law doesn't define 'adequate remedy.' It doesn't define 'justice.' It defines procedure. Ghana still went home."

**2:45–3:00 | Close**
"Football law doesn't need better data. It was designed to need better judgment. And it put that judgment in a room — the VAR room — where the people making the call are using words that have no definitions. That's what we built."

## 4. The Ruthless Cut Hierarchy
If the presentation is running long, cut in this order:
1. **Mbappé entirely** (saves ~15s).
2. **VAR nested layer** (saves ~20s).
3. **De Jong Decision 2** (saves ~5s).
4. **De Jong entirely** (saves ~25s).
**Never cut:** Perišić reveal or Suárez closer.

## 5. What Gets Simplified for the Demo
- **Perspectives:** Show only the 2-3 that disagree most sharply. Do not walk through all five.
- **Aggregate Data:** Cut entirely. The user's shifting judgment is the proof.
- **Reflection Text:** The reveal itself acts as the reflection.
